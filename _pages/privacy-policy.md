### What User Data We Collect

We do not collect personal user data **BUT** we use the following *third-party cookies* to display fonts, maths as well as the cookie consent popup:
- cdnjs.cloudflare.com
- cdn.jsdelivr.net
- cdn.mathjax.org
- use.fontawesome.com
